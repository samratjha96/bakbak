Restaurant Vocabulary

Today

Key phrases for ordering food:

  • おすすめ (osusume) - recommendation
  • これ/それ/あれ (kore/sore/are) - this/that/that over there
  • お願いします (onegaishimasu) - please
  • いくらですか (ikura desu ka) - how much is it?
Japanese Food Restaurant

Pronunciation Tips

Yesterday

Remember to pronounce the "r" sound in Japanese more like a light "d" + "r" blend. Practice:

  • りんご (ringo) - apple
  • 料理 (ryouri) - cooking
  • レストラン (resutoran) - restaurant
Japanese Pronunciation

Grammar Pattern

3 days ago

Question format: [Noun] は [Noun] ですか?

Example: これはメニューですか? (Is this the menu?)

Use the particle "は" (wa) to mark the topic, and end with ですか (desu ka) to form a question.
